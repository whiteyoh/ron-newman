# Vision Statement

We believe everyone should be able to understand, evaluate, and apply AI with confidence.

Our vision is to make practical AI literacy accessible through clear stages, hands-on examples, and real-world workflows—so learners, builders, and teams can move from curiosity to capability.

By turning complex concepts into tangible learning paths, this project aims to help people:

- Build a strong mental model of how AI systems mature
- Make better decisions about designing and using AI systems
- Create responsible, effective, and human-centered AI solutions

We envision a future where AI knowledge is not gated by jargon or credentials, but shared openly, learned progressively, and used to create meaningful impact.
