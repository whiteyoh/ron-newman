# Glytch AI Literacy Quiz

## AI beyond the chat box

Use this quiz at the end of a Glytch session to check understanding.

The aim is not to test whether someone can remember every level. The aim is to check whether they understand how to use AI safely, thoughtfully and with human judgement.

## How to use this quiz

- Give the quiz after the Glytch demo or workshop.
- Students or users can answer individually or in pairs.
- Allow 10 to 15 minutes.
- Use the answer guide at the end for marking or discussion.
- A suggested pass mark is 7 out of 10.

## Answer guide

### 1. Correct answer: B

AI should support learning, not replace thinking.

### 2. Example answer

AI can sound confident even when it is wrong, incomplete or making assumptions.

### 3. Correct answer: C

Do not enter personal pupil information, sensitive school data or private information.

### 4. Correct answers

```text
Prompt = B
Check = C
Evidence = A
Review = D
```

### 5. Example answer

AI help means using AI for ideas, explanations or improvements while still checking and understanding the work. Copying means using the output as your own without checking, understanding or doing the thinking.

### 6. Correct answer: A

Use the simplest AI support that safely gets the job done.

### 7. Example answer

Evidence matters because it helps you see what the answer is based on and whether it can be checked.

### 8. Correct answers: A, B and C

Human judgement is needed for checking, deciding suitability and reviewing important work.

### 9. Correct answer: B

More moving parts create more need to understand, check and review the result.

### 10. Example answers

Accept any two sensible checks, such as:

- whether the plan fits the time available
- whether the subjects and topics are correct
- whether breaks are included
- whether the plan is realistic
- whether anything important is missing
- whether it follows school or teacher guidance
