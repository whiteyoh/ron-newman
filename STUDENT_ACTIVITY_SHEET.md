# Glytch Student Activity Sheet

## AI beyond the chat box

AI can give quick answers, but quick answers are not always good answers.

In this activity, you will look at how AI work changes when checks, evidence and human review are added.

## By the end of this session, you will be able to:

- explain what changes when AI work has more steps
- spot what needs checking before using an AI answer
- decide when a simple answer is enough
- explain why your own thinking still matters

## Before we start

Remember:

- Do not enter personal information.
- Do not enter private school information.
- Do not copy AI answers without checking them.
- AI can help you think, but it should not do your thinking for you.
